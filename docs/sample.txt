Acme Corp Employee Handbook

Vacation Policy
Employees are entitled to 15 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation days must be approved by your manager at least 2 weeks in advance. Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year up to a maximum of 5 days.

Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work must be approved by your department head. Employees are expected to be available during core hours of 10am to 3pm in their local timezone.

Expense Policy
Business expenses up to $50 can be reimbursed without prior approval. Expenses above $50 require manager approval before purchase. All expense claims must be submitted within 30 days with receipts.

Health Benefits
Acme Corp provides full medical, dental, and vision coverage for all full-time employees. Coverage begins on the first day of the month following your start date. Dependents can be added during open enrollment in November.
